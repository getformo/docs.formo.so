## EVM

Formo is committed to supporting builders on EVM. Formo supports 40+ EVM chains.

> Formo autocaptures wallet events on your website and app from page visit to transaction for all EVM chains. Wallet profiles are available for all major chains.
>
> Chain support may be needed for [contract events](https://docs.formo.so/features/product-analytics/contract-events#contract-events) in funnels.
> [Book a chat](https://cal.com/formo/demo) if you have queries on chain support.
