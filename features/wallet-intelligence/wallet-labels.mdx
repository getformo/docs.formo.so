- Gamer
- Perp DEX User
- Top 10% Base User
- NFT Whale
- ETH Staker
- Liquid Staker
- Pudgy Holder

This helps you understand your users interests and behaviour. Wallet labels help you target qualified users who in your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP.)
