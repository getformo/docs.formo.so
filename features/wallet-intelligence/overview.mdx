## Features

Wallet intelligence helps you **understand and target users in web3**: 

- 📈 <a href="/features/wallet-intelligence/users">**Know your users.**</a> Capture wallet addresses of visitors and users in real-time.
- 🏃‍♀️‍➡️ <a href="/features/wallet-intelligence/audience-insights">**Audience insights.**</a> View your users' token holdings and top apps. Uncover users using similar apps.
- 🕵️‍♀️ <a href="/features/wallet-intelligence/wallet-profiles">**Wallet profiles.**</a> Turn anonymous wallets into high-value users. Track usage of specific, high-value wallets on your dapp.
- 🧑‍🧒‍🧒 <a href="/features/wallet-intelligence/segments">**Segments.**</a> Define groups of users that share one or more properties.
- 🏷️ <a href="/features/wallet-intelligence/wallet-labels">**Wallet labels.**</a> Understand each user's interests and behaviour with wallet labels.
- 💬 <a href="/features/wallet-intelligence/wallet-messaging">**Wallet messaging.**</a> Send the right message to your target users with deep personalization and insights.
- 📊 <a href="/features/wallet-intelligence/wallet-score">**Wallet reputation score.**</a> Use autogenerated wallet labels, onchain attestations, and proof-of-personhood to segment and rank your users.

Profile data is available for querying and export in the [Wallet Profile API](/api-reference/overview#wallet-profile-api).
