title: 'Active Users'
description: 'View unique wallet addresses that visit your dapp.'
---

The Active Users page shows you **a list of unique wallet addresses that have visited your dapp**. 
Understand your users' quality and engagement levels at a glance.
 
Each user is assigned a set of tags ('New User', 'Returning User') that segments different user types. 
You can also see users' net worth, first seen, and last seen times.
