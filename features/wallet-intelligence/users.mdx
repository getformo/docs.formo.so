title: 'Active Users'
description: 'View unique wallet addresses that visit your dapp.'
---


See **a list of unique wallet addresses that have visited your dapp**
to understand your users' quality and engagement levels at a glance.
 
Each user is automatically assigned a set of tags ('New User', 'Returning User') that segments them into different user types.

</Frame>

Clicking on a user will bring you to the user's <a href="/features/wallet-intelligence/wallet-profiles">Wallet Profile</a> page.
