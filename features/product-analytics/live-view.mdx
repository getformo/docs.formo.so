See a realtime activity feed of your current visitors on a rotating globe. Perfect for a mission control dashboard.

<Frame caption="Live view of your current visitors.">
	<img src="/images/live.png" alt="Live View" />
</Frame>

Click on any user or activity in the live feed to see where they are on the map. Click again to open their [wallet profile](/features/wallet-intelligence/wallet-profiles).

## When to use Live View

Live View is great for:

- **Launch day monitoring** - Watch users discover your app in real-time
- **Campaign monitoring** - See traffic spikes as campaigns go live
- **Demo dashboards** - Impress stakeholders with real-time activity
- **Incident response** - Quickly spot unusual patterns or errors

## How to use Live View

### Step 1: Open Live View

1. Go to the [Formo Dashboard](https://app.formo.so)
2. Select your project
3. Click **Overview** in the left navigation
4. Click **Current Visitors** to open Live View

<Frame caption="How to find Live view">
	<img src="/images/current-visitors.png" alt="Open Live view from the Current Visitors button" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Monitor activity

Events stream in real-time on an interactive globe:

- **Dots** represent user locations
- **Event cards** show recent activity
- Click any event to see full details
- Click a user to open their [wallet profile](/features/wallet-intelligence/wallet-profiles)

### Step 3: Use for presentations

Live View is perfect for team dashboards and presentations:

1. Open Live View in a browser tab
2. Expand to fullscreen mode
3. Display on a team monitor or during demos
