**Dapp analytics** gives you data superpowers to understand who is using your website and how it is being used: 

- 📊 <a href="#web3-growth-metrics">**Track web3 growth metrics.**</a> Track visitor counts, DAU, WAU, MAU, transactions, retention, and churn. Measure engagement and growth over time.
- 🏃‍♀️‍➡️ <a href="https://docs.formo.so/features/dapp-analytics/activity">**View a Real-time activity feed.**</a> See what users are doing in real-time. Uncover hidden drop-off points and retention drivers. 

### Web3 Growth Metrics

The Overview page shows you the key metrics for your dapp such as the number of visitors, wallets, and transactions.
With this data you can discover which tactics drive product adoption and growth.
