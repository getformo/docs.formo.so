Want feedback only from your actual token holders? Forms that verify onchain identity before submission? That's what token-gated forms do.

With Formo, you can build surveys, feedback forms, and applications that require wallet verification, specific NFT ownership, or World ID proof to submit. Only your real community members can respond, making your data more valuable and actionable.

## Prerequisites

- A Formo account with a connected project
- A smart contract deployed on a supported chain (Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, etc.)
- Basic knowledge of your token or NFT contract address and chain ID
- Optional: World ID approval for Sybil resistance

## Part 1: Navigate to Forms

Go to **Forms** in the sidebar (or visit `/teams/[teamId]/forms`). You'll see all your forms organized by status: PUBLISHED, DRAFT, and ARCHIVED.

Click **Create Form** in the top right.

## Part 2: Design Your Form

In the form builder, add your fields:

1. **Form Title & Description:** What's this form about? (e.g., "DAO Governance Feedback")
2. **Add Fields:** Click **Add Field** to create questions
   - Text input (short answers)
   - Long text (multi-line feedback)
   - Single select (multiple choice)
   - Multi-select (checkboxes)
   - Email
   - Rating scale
   - File upload (images, documents)

3. **Customize Branding:** Add your protocol's logo, colors, and background
4. **Theme:** Light or dark mode

Each field is optional. You can add as many fields as you need.

## Part 3: Set Up Token Gating

This is where the magic happens. Click the **Settings** tab, then **Token Gating**.

### Step 1: Choose Your Gating Type

- **ERC-20 Token:** Require minimum token balance (e.g., 100 USDC)
- **ERC-721 NFT:** Require specific NFT ownership (e.g., must hold your DAO NFT)
- **ERC-1155 Semi-Fungible:** Require specific token ID balance
- **Solana SPL Token:** Require minimum token balance on Solana

### Step 2: Enter Your Contract Details

1. **Contract Address:** Paste your token or NFT contract address (0x... format)
2. **Chain:** Select the blockchain (Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, BNB Chain, Solana, etc.)
3. **Minimum Balance:** How much do they need? (For ERC-721, set to 1 to require ownership)
4. **Token ID (ERC-1155 only):** Specify which token ID to check

### Step 3: Add Multiple Conditions (Optional)

Need to accept multiple token types? Click **Add Condition**:

- Require Ethereum NFT **OR** Polygon token
- Require governance token **AND** minimum balance of 1000
- Create complex onchain identity requirements

By default, all conditions use AND logic (users must meet every requirement). You can also configure OR logic by setting a minimum number of conditions that must be met.

## Part 4: Add World ID Verification (Optional)

For Sybil resistance, require World ID verification before form submission:

1. In Settings > Token Gating, toggle **Require World ID**
2. Users must verify with their World ID (Worldcoin) before submitting
3. This ensures one response per unique human, not per wallet

World ID is optional. You can require only token gating, only World ID, or both.

## Part 5: Preview and Publish

Before going live:

1. Click **Preview** to see what your community sees
2. Test the token gate (make sure your wallet meets requirements)
3. Test each form field to ensure it works

When ready, click **Publish**. Your form status changes to PUBLISHED and a public link is generated.

If you need to make changes, set it back to DRAFT, edit, then PUBLISH again.

## Part 6: Share Your Form

Your published form has a unique public URL. Copy it and share:

- Post on your website
- Share in Discord, Twitter, Telegram
- Include in emails to your community
- Embed on your landing page (iframe support coming)

When someone visits the link, they see:

1. **Step 1:** Wallet connect prompt
2. **Step 2:** Token verification (AI checks onchain balance)
3. **Step 3:** Form submission (if they meet requirements)
4. **Success:** Confirmation page

If they don't meet requirements, they see an error explaining what they need (e.g., "You need at least 100 tokens to respond").

## Part 7: View and Export Responses

As responses come in:

1. Click your published form from the list
2. See all submissions in the **Responses** tab
3. Click individual responses to view full details
4. Export responses as CSV for further analysis

Responses include:
- Timestamp of submission
- Wallet address that submitted
- All form field answers
- Token balance (at time of submission)
- World ID status (if required)

You can filter, sort, and export all responses.

## Use Case Examples

### DAO Governance Feedback
- **Gate:** Require 1+ governance token (ERC-20 or ERC-721)
- **Purpose:** Collect feedback from active governance members only
- **Form fields:** "Rate our current treasury strategy," "Which initiatives excite you?," "What should we build next?"

### NFT Holder Survey
- **Gate:** Require specific NFT collection (ERC-721)
- **Purpose:** Understand what your NFT holders want
- **Form fields:** "How satisfied are you with the collection?," "What features do you want?," "Would you buy more?"

### Airdrop Claim Form
- **Gate:** Require minimum token balance (ERC-20) + World ID verification
- **Purpose:** Prevent bots, ensure human claimants only
- **Form fields:** "What's your email for airdrop?," "How did you hear about us?," "Chain preference?"

### Beta Access Application
- **Gate:** Require wallet connect + custom questions
- **Purpose:** Recruit beta testers from your community
- **Form fields:** "Why do you want beta access?," "What's your web3 experience level?," "How often do you use our product?"

### Grant Application Form
- **Gate:** Require 10+ tokens (commitment filter)
- **Purpose:** Filter serious builders from tire kickers
- **Form fields:** "What are you building?," "How will you use our technology?," "What's your timeline?"

## Pro Tips

<Tip>
**Keep forms short.** 3-5 fields maximum. Longer forms = lower completion rates. Collect only what you actually need.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Make the gate clear upfront.** In your sharing message, tell people what they need: "You need at least 1 NFT from our collection to respond."
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Test with your own wallet first.** Submit a test response before publishing. Make sure the token gate actually works for you.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Use World ID for one-person-one-vote surveys.** If you're running important governance votes, require World ID to prevent sybil attacks (multiple wallets from same person).
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Export responses weekly.** Don't let responses pile up. Export and analyze them regularly to spot trends and feedback patterns.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Combine with your dashboard.** Import form responses as custom events into Formo analytics to correlate with onchain behavior.
</Tip>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which blockchains are supported?">
    Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, BNB Chain, Avalanche, Solana, and most EVM-compatible chains. Contact support if you need a specific chain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I require multiple token types (AND logic)?">
    Yes. Click "Add Condition" to require users to hold multiple tokens or NFTs. All conditions must be met to submit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if someone's balance changes after they submit?">
    Verification happens at submission time. If they have the required balance when they hit submit, their response is recorded. If their balance drops later, the submission still counts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to how many responses I can collect?">
    No limit. Collect as many responses as you want. Export anytime as CSV.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I embed my form on my website?">
    Yes, you can use the iframe embed code. Custom embedding options coming soon. Contact support for early access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if someone doesn't meet the token requirement?">
    They see a message explaining what they need. Example: "You need at least 100 USDC to respond to this form." They can't proceed until they acquire the required tokens.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does World ID cost anything?">
    World ID verification is free for you and your users. It's powered by Worldcoin and included in Formo forms.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change the gate after publishing?">
    Yes. Set the form back to DRAFT, edit the token gate, then PUBLISH again. Only new submissions after the change will use the new requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I prevent spam responses?">
    Token gating naturally filters spam since wallets need onchain assets. For extra protection, require World ID for one-person-one-vote verification.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I require specific NFT traits or attributes?">
    Not yet. We currently require any NFT from a contract. Custom trait checking is on our roadmap.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
